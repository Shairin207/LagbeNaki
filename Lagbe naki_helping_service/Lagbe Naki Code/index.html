FAQ

Are providers verified? ⌄

Yes. We check national ID and phone, review job history, and require 4.5 star ratings to stay active.

Which payments are supported? ⌄

bKash, Nagad, and debit/credit cards via a secure gateway. Cashless is preferred.

Can I reschedule? ⌄

Yes. You can reschedule or cancel up to 12 hours before the visit from your account page.

Does Deep Clean include Basic? ⌄

Yes—Deep Clean covers everything in Basic (dust & mop, bedroom & living area, trash removal) plus additional services: kitchen & bathroom focus, appliance wipe, and inside windows.

What if I’m not satisfied? ⌄

Tell us within 24 hours and we’ll arrange a free touch-up or a partial refund, depending on the case.
